Site Name:

The name of the site is Dulima Chamber of Commerce. Cacica Dulima was a representative woman leader from Tolima's región, which figth for our natives during the colonization period, so this name symbolizes the power of our women and how much can they do for our community. and the domind is dulimachamber.com

Site Purpose:

If you are a businesswoman that wants to join a community of powerful women building commercial business, and your business is located or offer its services in Ibagué Colombia, this website is for you. We are a chamber of commerce that provides a net of local businesswomen looking to make our business more visible, collaborate in services offered, teach each other strategies of growth, share interesting information and process before government entities' opportunities and the fulfillment of our rights.
